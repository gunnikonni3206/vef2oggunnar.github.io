A Reunion Beyond the Grave

Once a year on the Day of the Dead, it was said that the spirits of the departed would return to visit their loved ones. Among those eagerly waiting was Maria, who had lost her husband Carlos to a sudden illness. As the sun set, Maria set up an altar adorned with marigold flowers, candles, and Carlos's favorite foods. As night fell, she felt a gentle touch on her shoulder and turned to see Carlos smiling warmly at her. They embraced and spent the night reminiscing about their life together, until the first light of dawn, when Carlos bid her a tender goodbye until the next year.

The Lost Child of Dia de los Muertos

In a small village, there lived a young boy named Diego, who had never met his grandparents as they had passed away before he was born. However, on one Day of the Dead, as Diego was wandering through the cemetery, he suddenly found himself face to face with his grandmother. She took him by the hand and they strolled through the village, as she shared stories of her life and her love for him. As the day ended, she guided him back to the cemetery, leaving him with a small locket containing a picture of her, so he would always remember the bond they shared.
